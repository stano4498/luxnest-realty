Frequently Asked Questions

Buying Property

What is the first step in buying a home?

Get pre-approved for a mortgage and consult a real estate agent to understand your buying power and market options.

What are closing costs?

Closing costs are fees paid at the end of a transaction, including legal, title, and lender fees.

Selling Property

How do I determine the value of my home?

Use our free home valuation tool or request a professional CMA (Comparative Market Analysis) from an agent.

How long does it take to sell a property?

It varies by market, but typically 30-90 days from listing to closing with proper pricing and staging.

Renting Property

What documents do I need to rent a property?

You usually need identification, proof of income, references, and sometimes a credit report.

Are pets allowed in rentals?

This depends on the property owner. Always check the lease agreement before signing.

Real Estate Glossary

Appraisal

An estimate of a property's value conducted by a licensed professional.

Escrow

A financial arrangement where a third party holds funds until conditions of the sale are met.

Equity

The difference between the market value of a property and what is owed on the mortgage.
